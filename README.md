Online Examination Platform is a full-stack web application designed to conduct and manage exams digitally. It provides a secure and efficient way for institutions to organize exams, evaluate performance, and deliver instant results.
<br> <hr>
#Features <br>
1.User Authentication <br>
2.Secure login & signup <br>
3.Role-based access (Admin / Student) <br>
#Exam Management <br>
1.Create, update, and delete exams<br>
2.Add MCQ-based questions <br>
#Timer-Based Exams <br>
1.Auto-submit on time completion <br>
#Instant Results <br>
1.Automatic evaluation <br>
2.Score display after submission <br>
#Question Bank <br>
1.Organized question storage <br>
#Security <br>
1.Session handling <br>
2.Basic validation <br>
